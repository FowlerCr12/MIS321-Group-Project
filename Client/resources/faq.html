Frequently Asked Questions
Profile

1. What types of pets do you train?

We specialize in training dogs and cats of all breeds and ages. If you have another type of pet, feel free to contact us to see how we can help.

2. What training methods do you use?

Our approach is based on positive reinforcement. We focus on rewarding good behavior to build trust, confidence, and lasting results.There are no harsh techniques involved.

3. Do you offer private or group sessions?

Yes! We offer both private one-on-one sessions and small group classes to suit different learning needs and schedules.

4. How long does it take to see results?

While every pet is different, many owners start to see progress after just a few sessions. Continued practice at home plays a big role in long-term success.

5. Can you help with specific behavior problems?

Definitely. We handle issues like excessive barking, jumping, leash reactivity, aggression, house training, and more.

6. What should I bring to a training session?

Bring your pet on a leash or harness, some treats, and any questions you have. For group classes, a towel or mat is helpful too.

7. Do you offer training virtually or only at your facility?

We only offer on-site training options!
